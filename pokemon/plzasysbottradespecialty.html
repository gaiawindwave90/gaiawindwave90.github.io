This is a page for Pokémon Legends: Z-A players, so LISTEN UP!

A paid Nintendo Switch Online membership is required for the Link Play feature!

  • To unlock the Link Play feature, proceed the story until you meet Mable in the Research Lab.
  • Access the menu by pressing the X button during gameplay.
  • Select Link Play.
  • Then, select Link Trade!
  • Choose "Faraway Players" and connect the internet.
  • Enter the 8-digit code that GenPKM website gives you.

And there you go! You can only do three trades every 1 hour. You also must sign in on Google to do so.
